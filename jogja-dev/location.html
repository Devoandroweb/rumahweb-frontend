How to Get There

By TransJogja

The closest bus stop for the TransJogja is near Mirot Kampus and in front of Panti Rapih Hospital (Jl. Cik Ditiro)

By Taxi/Grab/Gojek

The most convenient drop-off location is in front of GIK Building on Jalan Pancasila (UGM Boulevard)

By Car/Motorcycle

Free parking available on campus. Enter through the parking gate on Jl. Persatuan and follow signs to GIK building.
